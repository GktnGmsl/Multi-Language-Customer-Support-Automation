ShopVista SHIPPING POLICY
=========================
Last updated: March 2026

This page describes all shipping, delivery, and order tracking conditions for
ShopVista Elektronik ve Yaşam A.Ş. (shopvista.com.tr).


1. SHIPPING PARTNERS
--------------------
ShopVista works with the following carriers for domestic shipments in Turkey:

  - Yurtiçi Kargo
  - Aras Kargo
  - MNG Kargo
  - PTT Kargo (for remote areas and post-office pickup preference)

For international shipments, we partner with DHL Express and UPS. Orders are automatically
assigned to the carrier that provides the fastest delivery to your location.


2. DELIVERY TIMEFRAMES
-----------------------
DOMESTIC (Turkey)
  - Major cities (Istanbul, Ankara, Izmir, Bursa, Antalya, etc.) : 2–3 business days
  - Other provinces                                               : 3–5 business days
  - Remote / rural areas and island destinations                  : 5–7 business days

INTERNATIONAL
  - European countries          : 7–10 business days
  - USA and Canada              : 10–15 business days
  - Rest of the world           : 10–20 business days

Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding public holidays. During peak periods
(campaigns, holidays, etc.) delivery times may be extended; the updated estimated delivery
date will appear in your order confirmation e-mail.


3. SHIPPING RATES
-----------------
DOMESTIC

  Order Value              | Shipping Fee
  -------------------------|-------------
  Below 500 TRY            | 29.90 TRY
  500 TRY and above        | FREE

  Same-day delivery (Istanbul European Side only, for orders placed before 11:00 AM on
  weekdays): additional 49.90 TRY surcharge applies.

INTERNATIONAL

  Order Value              | Shipping Fee
  -------------------------|-------------
  Below 75 USD             | 15.00 USD
  75 USD and above         | FREE

  All international orders are shipped DDP (Delivered Duty Paid). Customs duties and
  import taxes are covered by ShopVista — you will not be charged additional fees upon
  delivery. Some countries with special import regulations may be excluded; see the
  checkout page for country-specific information.


4. ORDER PROCESSING & DISPATCH TIMES
--------------------------------------
  - Orders placed before 14:00 (UTC+3) on weekdays are dispatched the same day.
  - Orders placed after 14:00 or on weekends are dispatched the next business day.
  - Out-of-stock items show an estimated restock date on the product page.
  - Personalized or custom-made items require 3–5 business days before dispatch.


5. ORDER TRACKING
-----------------
Once your order is dispatched you will receive a tracking number by e-mail and SMS.

Tracking options:
  a) shopvista.com.tr → "My Orders" → Select order → "Track Shipment" button
  b) Visit the carrier's website and enter your tracking number.
  c) WhatsApp: +90 850 123 45 67 (24/7 chatbot, live support during business hours)


6. DELIVERY ISSUES
------------------
MISSED DELIVERY
  If the courier cannot reach you, a notification will be left at your door and the parcel
  will be held at the nearest carrier branch. If not collected within 3 business days, the
  parcel is returned to ShopVista. A re-shipping fee of 29.90 TRY (domestic) or 15 USD
  (international) will apply for re-dispatch.

DELAYED DELIVERY
  If your order is at least 2 business days past the estimated delivery date, contact us at
  support@shopvista.com.tr with your order number. We will open a trace with the carrier
  and provide an update within 5 business days.

LOST PARCEL
  If the carrier issues a loss report, ShopVista will either resend the item free of charge
  or issue a full refund — whichever the customer prefers.

DAMAGED PARCEL
  If the package is visibly damaged on arrival, document the damage in front of the courier
  before signing and report it to ShopVista immediately. Claims submitted without a courier
  damage record may be denied by the carrier.


7. INVOICE INFORMATION
-----------------------
  - An electronic invoice (e-Invoice or e-Archive) is sent by e-mail upon order completion.
  - For corporate invoices, enter your tax ID and company name during checkout.
    Invoice details cannot be changed after an order is placed.
