ShopVista RETURNS & REFUNDS POLICY
===================================
Last updated: March 2026

At ShopVista Elektronik ve Yaşam A.Ş., customer satisfaction is our top priority.
If you are not completely happy with your purchase for any reason, you may return it
under the conditions set out below.


1. RETURN WINDOW
----------------
You have 14 (fourteen) calendar days from the date of delivery to initiate a return
request. This period is aligned with the right of withdrawal granted under Turkish
Consumer Protection Law No. 6502, and applies to all orders regardless of shipping
destination. Return requests submitted after the 14-day window will not be accepted.


2. RETURN CONDITIONS
--------------------
For a return to be accepted, all of the following conditions must be met:

  a) The item must be unused and returned in its original packaging.
  b) All accessories, cables, manuals, and any bundled gifts must be included.
  c) All labels and tags on the product must remain intact and attached.
  d) The original invoice or receipt must be included in the return shipment.
  e) The product must not show any physical damage, scratches, or breakage.
  f) For electronics, the serial number must not be altered, covered, or removed.


3. HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
----------------------------
Step 1: Log in to shopvista.com.tr and go to "My Orders".
Step 2: Select the order you wish to return and click "Request a Return".
Step 3: Choose your return reason and add any additional comments if applicable.
Step 4: A confirmation e-mail with a prepaid return shipping label will be sent to you.
         Attach the label to the parcel and drop it off at the designated carrier location.
         (ShopVista covers domestic return shipping costs free of charge.)
Step 5: Once the item reaches our warehouse, inspection is completed within 2 business days.
Step 6: You will be notified by e-mail with the inspection result. If approved, the
         refund process is initiated immediately.


4. REFUND PROCESS
-----------------
  - Credit / Debit card: Refunded within 10 business days of return approval. Your bank
    may take up to 14 business days to post the credit to your account.
  - Bank transfer (wire): Refunded within 5 business days to the IBAN on your account.
  - ShopVista Wallet: Credited within 1 business day.

The full product price is refunded with no deductions.


5. NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS (EXCEPTIONS)
--------------------------------------
The following categories are excluded from the return policy:

  - Opened, installed, or activated software and digital licenses
  - Unsealed underwear, swimwear, bikinis, and other intimate / personal hygiene items
  - Personalized or custom-made items (e.g., name-printed or engraved products)
  - Opened cosmetics and personal care products
  - Expired or perishable food products
  - Downloaded digital content (music, films, e-books) once accessed or downloaded
  - Opened medical and hygiene supplies (masks, syringes, etc.)
  - Special-order items not held in standard inventory

These exceptions are in line with Article 15 of the Turkish Right of Withdrawal
Regulation (Mesafeli Sözleşmeler Yönetmeliği).


6. DAMAGED OR INCORRECT ITEMS
------------------------------
If your item arrives damaged or incorrect, document the issue in the presence of the
courier before signing for delivery. If you notice damage after opening, report it
within 48 hours by e-mailing support@shopvista.com.tr with photos and your order number.
The 14-day return window does not apply to damaged or incorrect items; these are
handled on a priority basis with a free replacement or full refund.


7. INTERNATIONAL RETURNS
-------------------------
For international return shipments, please contact support@shopvista.com.tr first to
receive a return merchandise authorization (RMA) number and shipping instructions.
International return shipping costs vary by parcel size and destination. ShopVista will cover
return shipping costs entirely if the return is due to a product defect or our error.
